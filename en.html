Tourism in Panfilov District

Panfilov District is a unique region that combines historical monuments, scenic natural landscapes, and a rich culture. The district offers tourists unforgettable routes through ancient landmarks and the opportunity to explore local traditions.

About Panfilov District

History of the District: Panfilov District is a region with a rich history, founded several centuries ago. It plays an important role as a cultural and trade center, located on one of the routes of the Great Silk Road.

Key Facts:

  • Population: 130,000 people
  • Geographical Location: Panfilov District is located in southeastern Kazakhstan, bordering China.
  • Climate: Continental, with hot summers and cold winters.

The district attracts tourists with its natural attractions, including picturesque mountains, gorges, and rich cultural heritage.

Places to Visit in Panfilov District

Zharkent Mosque-Pagoda

A unique architectural monument combining elements of Chinese and Muslim cultures. Located in the city of Zharkent, this mosque is a vibrant example of Eastern architectural traditions.

Aulie-Agash

A sacred tree located near Zharkent, one of the most famous natural and spiritual landmarks of the region. "Aulie-Agash" means "Holy Tree," and it is believed to possess magical and healing properties.

Zharkent-Arasan

A balneological resort known for its mineral springs. Located near Zharkent, it attracts tourists with its therapeutic waters, which are believed to treat various ailments.

Kerim-Agash

Another sacred tree in the vicinity of Panfilov District, known for its spiritual traditions and energy, drawing both pilgrims and tourists.

Prophet Elijah's Church

A historic church located in the Panfilov District, this site is an important religious and cultural landmark that attracts many tourists and pilgrims.

Kasteev Museum

A museum dedicated to the art and cultural heritage of Kazakhstan. It features works by Kazakh artist Abylkhan Kasteev and other notable artists.

Ili River

A beautiful river flowing through Panfilov District, it is a popular spot for fishing, walking, and water activities for tourists and locals alike.

Tiger Mountains

Picturesque mountains known for their unique nature and stunning views. This place is perfect for hiking and enjoying nature.

Singing Dune

A natural phenomenon — a sand dune that emits a sound resembling singing. Located in the "Altyn Emel" National Park, it attracts tourists with its uniqueness.

Useful Information for Tourists

Visa

Before traveling to the Panfilov district, check the visa requirements for your country. Many countries have the option of visa-free entry or can obtain a visa upon arrival.

Weather

The climate of the Panfilov district is continental. Summer months are warm, while winter months are cold. It is recommended to check the weather forecast before your trip.

Currency Exchange Rate

The exchange rate of the tenge (KZT) against major currencies can be found at local exchange offices or banks. It is advisable to exchange money at official places for a better rate.

Transport Infrastructure

In the Panfilov district, bus routes and taxis are available. You can also rent a car for convenience in getting around the area and its surroundings.

Language

The official languages are Kazakh and Russian. In major tourist areas, many people speak English, but it is useful to know a few phrases in Kazakh or Russian for communication with locals.

Tourist Attractions

The Panfilov district is famous for its natural attractions, including national parks and cultural monuments. Don't forget to visit Altyn Emel and other nature reserves.
